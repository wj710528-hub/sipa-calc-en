On-screen luminance guide (engineering reference)

40–60 nitCinema / dark room (about 12–18 fL; 14 fL ≈ 48 nit)
60–120 nitHome theater / dim room
120–200 nitMeeting or classroom / controlled ambient light
200–300 nitBright indoor / exhibit
>300 nitSpecialty projection: verify ambient light, screen and system contrast
Note: This is a theoretical estimate based on rated ISO lumens, image area and nominal screen gain. Actual on-screen luminance also depends on calibrated output, lens setting, screen properties, ambient light, uniformity, light-source aging and installation conditions. In bright environments, image-system contrast must also be verified.
